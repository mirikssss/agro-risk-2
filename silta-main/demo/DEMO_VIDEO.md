# Demo Video Instructions

## Recording Requirements

Please record a demo video (1-5 minutes) that shows:

### Required Content:

1. **Introduction (Optional - 1 minute pitch)**
   - Brief overview of Silta
   - The problem it solves
   - Why it matters

2. **Key Features Demonstration (2-4 minutes)**
   - Show the main navigation and sections
   - Demonstrate face recognition in action (if possible with sample images)
   - Show how the app displays names and relationships
   - Highlight the privacy-first approach
   - Show the simple, accessible interface

3. **Technical Highlights**
   - Mention the on-device AI processing
   - Show the offline functionality
   - Demonstrate the user-friendly design

### Recording Tips:

- Use screen recording software (OBS, QuickTime, or similar)
- Speak clearly and explain what you're showing
- Prioritize key flows and features
- Keep the video between 1-5 minutes
- Ensure good audio quality
- Use clear, readable text in the UI

### Where to Upload:

1. Upload to YouTube, Vimeo, or similar platform
2. Update the link in `/demo/README.md` under "Demo Recording" section
3. Ensure the video is publicly accessible (no login required)

### Example Structure:

```
[0:00-0:30] Introduction and problem statement
[0:30-2:00] Main features walkthrough
[2:00-3:30] Face recognition demonstration
[3:30-4:00] Privacy and technology highlights
[4:00-4:30] Conclusion and next steps
```
